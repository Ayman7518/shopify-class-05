FROM THE BEGINNING

Our Coffee Story is much wider,much higher and much deeper than meets
the eye.we began making coffee at seven Wonders the first week of
January 2003 on our first espresso machine.Less than 3 months later we
had won the Irish Barista Championship (the main coffee making
competition in the world by country) abd were off to Boston to represent
Ireland in the World Barista Championship
